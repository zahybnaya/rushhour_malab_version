Instructions - Computing your bonus

When you are done (either when your time is up or when you solved all puzzles) we are going to randomly choose three puzzles (out of the 70 puzzles).

We then calculate your bonus based on how many of these three randomly selected puzzles you solved.

Note: We are going to treat in the same manner puzzles you surrendered and puzzles you did not get to do for lack of time. So try to complete as many puzzles as you can to increase the chances of a high bonus.

Number of puzzles completed Bonus Total
0 $0 $1
1 $3 $4
2 $6 $7
3 $9 $10

Make sure you COMPLETELY understand the requirements and how your bonus will be calculated!

Click "I UNDERSTAND" when you are ready.
