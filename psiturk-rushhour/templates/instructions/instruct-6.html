Instructions - Computing your bonus

When you are done (either when your time is up or when you solved all puzzles) we are going to randomly choose three puzzles (out of the 70 puzzles).

We then calculate your bonus based on how many of these selected three puzzles you completed successfully.

Note: We are going to treat puzzles you surrendered and puzzles you did not get to do for lack of time in the same manner.
