Instructions - How to not get your HIT rejected

There are 70 puzzles to solve. Your allotted time for solving these puzzles is 60 minutes.

You are allowed to finish the last puzzle you play. Try to solve as many puzzles as you can since this would affect your bonus (see next page).

Important: We are expecting you to complete at least 8 puzzles. Failing to complete the minimum number of puzzles will result in REJECTING your HIT. But don't worry, solving the minimum number of puzzles should not be too difficult.
