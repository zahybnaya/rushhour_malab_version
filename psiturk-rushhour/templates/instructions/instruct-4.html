Restart and surrender

You are going to play for 45 minutes. Try to solve as many puzzles as you can. You are allowed to finish the last game you play.

It is important to us that you will try your best, but occusionaly you might find a puzzle that will be difficult for you to solve.

In that case you can decide to hit the RESTART button which will get you back to the initial position of the puzzle. If you see no other option, you can also use the SURRENDER button, which will get you to the next puzzle.

NOTE: All puzzles are solvable.

Both buttons are located on the top part of the screen.
