Restart and surrender

You are going to play for 45 minutes.

You are allowed to finish the last game you play. Try to solve as many as you can. All puzzles are solvable.

It is important to us that you will try your best, but occusionaly you might find a puzzle that will be difficult for you to solve. In that case you can decide to hit the RESTART button which will get you back to the initial position of the puzzle. You can also decide to hit the SURRENDER button, which will get you to the next puzzle.

Both buttons are located on the top part of the screen.
