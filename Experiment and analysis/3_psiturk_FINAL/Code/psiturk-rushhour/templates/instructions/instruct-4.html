Instructions - Restart and surrender

It is important to us that you will try your best, but occasionally you might find a puzzle that will be difficult for you to solve.

In that case you can decide to hit the RESTART button which will get you back to the initial position of the puzzle.
As a last resort, you can also use the SURRENDER button, which will get you to the next puzzle.

The Surrender button is going to be disabled for the first few moves (marked with a red cross) in order to encourage you to try to solve the puzzle.

NOTE: All puzzles are solvable.

Both buttons are located on the top part of the screen.
