Instructions - How to not get your HIT rejected

There are 70 puzzles to solve. Your allotted time for solving these puzzles is 60 minutes (120 minutes are allotted to the HIT so you can take plenty of breaks). You can also try to solve all puzzles (and complete the task) before the time is up.

You are allowed to finish the last puzzle you play. Try to solve as many puzzles as you can since this will affect your bonus (see next page).

Important: We are expecting you to try and solve as many puzzles as you can. Stopping to solve the puzzles could result in REJECTING your HIT. Make sure you keep trying for the entire 60 minutes.
